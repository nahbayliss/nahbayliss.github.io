PROJECTS I'VE WORKED ON

Here is a selection of my work that I am most proud of. I can work on a range of projects requiring different skills, and I am always aiming to improve. I have technical coding skills (HTML, CSS, C#), and I am experienced in all industry-standard applications required to get work done to a high standard. I love all areas of design but my favourite aspects would be web design and app design. If I sound like the perfect fit for your project, shoot me an email down at the bottom of the page!

MyLocalMind Inc. Website Redesign

MyLocalMind Inc is a community wellbeing and mental health charity, and they aim to cater to the needs of their local community. They provide services that are either inaccessible due to cost or are unavailable to the community.

Murdoch Futurism Showcase 2020

Murdoch University’s End-Of-Year Showcase is a recurring event every year, and 2020’s theme that we designed was “Futurism”. This project taught students about what it is like to work in a large service design project, and there were over 50 students working on Futurism.

likeMinded. App Design

This app concept that I created, likeMinded., was in response to the lack of students that were involved with Murdoch University’s many clubs. This app would allow students to find clubs that aligned with their interests and find all the necessary information that they could want to know about a specific club.

Portfolio Project Strategy

In the planning stage of creating both this website and my printed portfolio, I put all my planning and research together into a concise document, my portfolio project strategy. This would be the outlining document for what my portfolio would become upon completion.

Guiding Design Print Guide

Guiding Design is a guide that teaches designers all about print. This involves the history of print and typography, colour schemes, how to pair fonts, how to set up a document, different print specialties and paper stocks, and more.

Sophie's Sox Branding

Sophie’s Sox is a sock brand run by Sophie, a young lady with Autism and Down Syndrome, situated in Fremantle, Western Australia. She runs a quirky sock business that sells uniquely dyed bamboo socks.

Character Armour Design

For this project I designed armour for a character. This could go in any design direction that I wanted, the end goal was to have a completed rendered pose of the character and a character turnaround.

VegOut Vegetarian Restaurant Locator

VegOut is a concept that I came up with from the personal struggle of someone who hasn’t eaten meat in a decade. People with dietary requirements struggle to find places to go out to eat that can cater to them and often feel like the odd-one-out in public with friends and family.

Little Red Riding Hood Illustration

For this project I re-imagined the story of Little Red Riding Hood to fit into a different context. I was inspired by early animation and the videogame Cuphead for this one.

Advanced Character Posing

For this project of portraying character expressions, I worked to familiarise myself with moving 3D rigs to convey convincing emotions. The purpose of this was to home in on how a character’s face and body would look when they feel a specific emotion, and how I would make that visually interesting to the viewer.

Snow Impressions Technical Art

In my playable Unity build “Snow Impressions”, I created something where the player can drive a car around on a snowy plane, and the wheels will leave tracks behind in the snow. This was created using Unity’s free assets, and myself coding in C#.

Architectual Visualisation

In these high-quality renders of two buildings, I created both traditional and modern architecture. This was created in Maya through 3D sculpting and texturing.
